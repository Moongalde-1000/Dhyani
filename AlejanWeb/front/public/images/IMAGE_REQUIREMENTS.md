# Image Assets Required for Digital Evento Homepage

## 📸 Required Images

### 1. Logo (logo.png)
**Location**: `images/logo.png`

**Specifications**:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Recommended Size: 180px × 60px (width × height)
- Content: "Digital Evento" logo from your brand assets
- The logo should match the one in the reference design

**How to add**:
1. Save your logo as `logo.png`
2. Place it in the `images/` folder
3. The header will automatically display it

---

### 2. Phone Mockup (phone-mockup.png)
**Location**: `images/phone-mockup.png`

**Specifications**:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Recommended Size: 600px × 1200px (or similar aspect ratio)
- Content: iPhone/smartphone mockup showing a digital invitation
- Should match the phone mockup from your reference design
- The phone should have a shadow/depth effect

**From your reference image**: 
- Black iPhone with rounded corners
- Screen showing colorful celebration/party invitation
- Tilted at a slight angle
- Dropped shadow for depth

**How to add**:
1. Save your phone mockup as `phone-mockup.png`
2. Place it in the `images/` folder
3. The hero section will automatically display it with floating animation

---

## 🎨 Design Notes

The CSS is already set up to:
- Scale images responsively on all devices
